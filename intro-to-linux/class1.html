Background of Linux

  • Based on UNIX operating system created by AT&T in 1969.
  • Created by Linus Torvalds in 1991, who was a student at University of Helsinki, Finland at the time. He also created the distributed version control system Git.
  • Open source code.
  • Which other operating system is based on UNIX, but is NOT open source?

Kernel

What is the kernel?

The kernel, think of it as a seed, is the heart of the computer's operating system, and for the most part has complete control over everything in the system.
